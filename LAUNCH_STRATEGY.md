# CleanDispatch Launch Strategy
## Getting Your First Clients & Cleaners

---

## Phase 1: Pre-Launch (Week 1)

### Build Your Cleaner Network First
**Why:** Cleaners → Capacity → Can accept clients → Revenue

**Action Items:**
- [ ] Recruit 10-15 cleaners (your network, referrals, Facebook groups)
- [ ] Get them signed up with references approved
- [ ] Have 2-week availability windows filled out
- [ ] Capacity target: 15-20 jobs/week

**Where to find cleaners:**
- Personal referrals (friends, family)
- Facebook: "Tampa Cleaning Community," "Hillsborough Jobs," "Apollo Beach Services"
- Nextdoor app (local residents)
- Craigslist (services section)
- Indeed (post job: "Independent Cleaning Contractor")

**Recruitment message:**
> "Earn $20/hr + $8/job with CleanDispatch. We auto-assign you cleaning jobs that fit YOUR schedule. Automatic payouts every Friday. No haggling with clients—we handle pricing, scheduling, everything."

### Set Cleaner Expectations
- You'll get 2-5 jobs/week (if available)
- First jobs = building reputation (5-star quality expected)
- Support: email, phone, SMS for any issues
- Onboarding required: references, availability, photos, bank info

---

## Phase 2: Soft Launch (Week 2)

### Target: First 5 Clients

**Marketing Channels (FREE):**

**1. Facebook Posts (Your Personal Account + Local Groups)**
```
"Tired of booking cleaners over the phone? CleanDispatch gives you 
instant quotes online. Pick your date & time. Professional cleaners 
backed by references. Book now: [link]"
```
- Post in: Hillsborough County groups, Apollo Beach community groups, Tampa area groups
- Post daily (5 different variations)

**2. Nextdoor App**
- Post in your neighborhood
- Target: Apollo Beach, Carrollwood (Hillsborough), Brandon
- Message: "Professional home cleaning. Instant quote online. No phone calls."

**3. Word of Mouth**
- Tell everyone you know (and their cleaning needs)
- Offer: First 5 clients get $10 discount (or "$10 off your first clean")
- Goal: Rapid word-of-mouth from satisfied early adopters

**4. Craigslist (Services)**
- Post in Tampa Bay "Services" section: "House Cleaning"
- Daily reposting (Craigslist buries old posts)
- Cost: ~$5/posting × 1-2 posts/week

**5. Google My Business**
- Create local business listing (Apollo Beach, Hillsborough, Manatee)
- Get 5+ reviews from early clients ASAP
- Google will surface you in searches for "house cleaning near me"

**Early Client Incentive:**
```
"First-time customer special: $15 off your first clean"
(Show discount code on booking page)
```
This creates urgency and drives word-of-mouth.

## Phase 3: Early Growth (Week 3-4)

### Target: Scale to 30-50 clients/month

**If Phase 2 succeeded (5+ bookings):**
- You have proof it works
- You have testimonials/5-star reviews
- Cleaners are getting repeat work = happy campers

**Now scale the free channels:**

**1. Double down on Facebook**
- Join 10+ local service groups
- Post testimonials/before-afters from first clients
- Encourage reviews: "Leave us 5 stars on Google → $5 off next clean"

**2. Google Ads (Paid - $5-10/day)**
- Target: "house cleaning near Apollo Beach"
- Target: "cleaning service Hillsborough County"
- Low spend, high conversion (search intent)
- Track ROI strictly

**3. Referral Program**
- Existing client refers friend → Both get $10 off next clean
- Cleaners refer new client → Cleaner gets $5 bonus
- Exponential growth through incentives

**4. Email Newsletter**
- If you collect emails, send weekly: "This month's specials," "Cleaner spotlight," "Cleaning tips"
- Keep top-of-mind for repeat bookings

**5. Local Partnerships (FREE)**
- Partner with local realtor offices
- Partner with property management companies
- Offer "Move-in cleaning special" (they recommend you)
- Free signage in their offices

## Phase 4: Sustainable Growth (Month 2+)

### Target: 80-150 bookings/month (recurring revenue)

**By now you should have:**
- 20-30 active cleaners

- 30-40% repeat clients (the gold mine)

1. **Google/SEO** - Organic (grows over time)
2. **Repeat clients** - 40-50% of revenue

4. **Facebook** - Continues to drive signups

**Expansion (after Month 2):**
- Move beyond Apollo Beach → Tampa, Carrollwood, Brandon
- Move beyond Hillsborough → Manatee County
- Add "commercial cleaning" if demand warrants

## Keys to Success

### For Clients:
1. **Speed** - Quote in 30 seconds
2. **Transparency** - Zero hidden fees
3. **Quality** - 5-star cleaners only
4. **Protection** - Auto-hold if job runs over

### For Cleaners:
1. **Consistency** - Regular job flow
2. **Simplicity** - No admin, just clean
3. **Fair pay** - $20/hr + gas
4. **Support** - Email/SMS for issues

### For YOU:
1. **Stay hands-off** - System runs itself
2. **Monitor quality** - 1x/week review of ratings
3. **Optimize channels** - Cut losers, double winners
4. **Keep customers happy** - 90% of revenue = repeat clients

---

## Red Flags & Fixes

| Problem | Solution |
|---------|----------|
| No clients signing up | ❌ Wrong messaging. Rewrite copy. Try new channels. |
| Cleaners declining jobs | ❌ Jobs don't match availability. Improve assignment logic. |
| Low ratings (< 4.5★) | ❌ Cleaner quality issue. Deactivate, retrain, or replace. |
| High payment holds | ❌ Quote formula too optimistic. Adjust multipliers. |
| Cleaners quitting | ❌ Poor job quality/pay. Check assignment logic. May need more manual oversight early. |
