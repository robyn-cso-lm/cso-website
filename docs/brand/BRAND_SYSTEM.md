# Brand System

## Source of truth

The complete human-facing system is in SharePoint:

`CSO Family / S Drive / CSO Group / Brand and Marketing / 00 Master Brand System`

Read this file before every visual, layout, asset, or copy change. Update the SharePoint master first when a brand decision changes.

## Canadian Surrogacy Options (CSO)

- Palette: Deep Purple `#3D1A6E`, Gold `#C8973A`, Lavender `#E8E0F5`, Near-White Cream `#FFFCF8`, Dark Plum `#1C0D30`.
- Type: Playfair Display or Cormorant Garamond for headlines; Instrument Sans, Jost, or Source Sans Pro for UI/body.
- Use the full dragonfly logo for formal and large-format work. Use the baby-in-shell icon only for favicon, app icon, avatar, or other small formats.
- Voice: warm, direct, reassuring, experienced. No em dashes.
- Canadian-facing surrogacy copy uses reimbursement language, not compensation language.

## Little Miracles (LM)

- Palette: Burgundy `#A41F35`, Dusty Pink `#E0ABAD`, Blush `#F0DDD8`, Soft Lavender `#C9B8E8`, Soft Aqua `#D8F1F0`, Warm Cream `#FFF9F6`, Body Gray `#646464`.
- Type: Gloock, Kepler, Playfair Display, or Cormorant Garamond for headlines; Source Sans Pro, Instrument Sans, or Work Sans for UI/body.
- Voice: gentle, hopeful, emotionally intelligent, never transactional.
- Script is for small accents only, never body copy.

## Camica

- Palette: Deep Teal `#0F4D4D`, Sea Teal `#2E6F6B`, Sage Teal `#77A9A6`, Sea Foam `#BED9D6`, Champagne Gold `#C8A66A`, Warm Beige `#E9DED2`, Ivory `#F7F4EF`, Charcoal `#23333B`.
- Type: Cormorant Garamond for headlines; Jost for UI/body.
- Use the teal/gold logo as the primary mark and the CE monogram only for favicon, avatar, or other small formats.
- Voice: confident, intimate, polished, direct, and calm. Never hype.

## Non-negotiable rules

- These are distinct brands. Never mix CSO purple into Camica or Camica teal into LM.
- Never invent colours, fonts, logos, program names, pricing, timelines, matches, outcomes, or legal results.
- Preserve existing behaviour and do not redesign unrelated screens.
- Prefer real stories, founder perspective, and human proof over generic stock language.
- Keep layouts clean, spacious, readable, and mobile-first.
