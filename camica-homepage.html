The Camica Hybrid Pathway

Already waiting
in Canada?

Canada's altruistic-only surrogacy law means deep waitlists and no clear timeline. The Hybrid Pathway is Camica's answer: a Florida-based gestational carrier who travels to Ontario for delivery. You stay home. Your baby is born in Canada. And you don't wait 18 more months.

01
You sign with Camica. We begin surrogate matching from our Florida pool, typically within weeks.
02
Your surrogate carries in Florida. All medical appointments, legal contracts, and support are managed by Camica.
03
She travels to Ontario for delivery. Your baby is born in Canada. Your legal team handles the birth order on both sides of the border.
04
You go home with your family. Camica handles post-birth coordination and ongoing support.

Surrogate Requirements

Are you a good candidate?

We're looking for real women, not perfect ones. If you check most of these boxes, let's talk.

  • Age 21 – 40
  • At least one prior healthy full-term pregnancy
  • Done having your own children
  • BMI under 32
  • Non-smoker; no illicit drug use
  • US citizen, permanent resident, or valid visa
  • Stable home environment and support system
  • No disqualifying medical conditions
