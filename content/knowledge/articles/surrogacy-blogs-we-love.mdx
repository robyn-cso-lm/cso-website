We have our own blog because we like to be able to share information and stories with our CSO family. Of course, there are many other blogs out there as well that are related to surrogacy and provide an excellent amount of literature from helping you with your decision to become a surrogate, intended parent(s) or even provide with helpful information for your journey with surrogacy.

We decided to provide you with a list of surrogacy blogs we think are great. Check them out below:

All Things Surrogacy

This well-rounded surrogacy blog highlights couples, surrogates and agencies to provide you with a huge source of information for first-hand experience with surrogacy. They also feature other surrogate bloggers as well!

Our Misconception

This amazing and honest couple share their experience with infertility and parenthood. It’s a great blog to read to learn that you are not alone in this!

Their Bun, My Oven

A blog written first-hand by a surrogate. She openly shares her experience as a surrogate (just like our awesome guest blogger!)

Chrissy & Lee…Our Surrogacy Journey

Want to hear the intended parent’s side of the story? Read about their experience and check our their photos and video content!

My Body. Their Baby

This is the story of a 2x surrogate! The blog has not been updated in well over a year, but it’s still a great read!

We love these blogs because they each tell a story from their own experience. Whether you making a decision, or currently experiencing surrogacy, reading the experience of others can help you relate and gain more insight. You get to know the couple or surrogate and read about their journey. Some of the blogs are a little dated, but still give you aa great read if you start from the beginning.

We try to incorporate as much experience as possible into our own blog, but we also know that there is so much more out there to help you with your own journey. Do you have any other surrogacy blogs you love to read? Tell us in the comments below!
