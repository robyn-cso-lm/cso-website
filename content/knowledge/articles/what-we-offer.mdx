What We Offer to Intended Parents

At

Canadian Surrogacy Options Inc. (CSO)

, we understand that embarking on a surrogacy journey is a significant and emotional decision. With over 30 years of experience as Canada’s first and longest-running surrogacy agency, we are committed to providing comprehensive support tailored to your unique path to parenthood.:contentReference[oaicite:11]{index=11}

Our Comprehensive Services Include:

Personalized Consultation:

A dedicated one-on-one free consultation to address all your questions and concerns.

Profile Development:

Assistance in creating and refining your Intended Parent profile, with unlimited reviews to ensure it reflects your story.

Community Support:

Access to our private Intended Parent Facebook support group, connecting you with others on similar journeys.

Professional Referrals:

Connections to experienced lawyers, clinics, and counselors to guide you through the legal and medical aspects.

Egg Donation Services:

Special referral to our sister company,

Little Miracles Inc.

, for egg donation options.

Surrogate Matching:

Circulation of your profile to available surrogates, facilitating meaningful connections.

Logistical Coordination:

Assistance with booking appointments, travel, and accommodations for both you and your surrogate.

Ongoing Communication:

Regular updates and consistent communication throughout your journey, celebrating milestones together.

Financial Management:

Complete management of reimbursements, including sorting and filing all surrogate receipts and documentation.

Surrogate Support:

Assignment of an individual support worker for your surrogate, ensuring she receives dedicated care.

Relationship Mediation:

Mediation services to foster a positive and supportive connection between you and your surrogate.

In-Person Support:

Availability for in-person support during screening and transfer appointments, as permitted.

Advocacy:

Continuous advocacy for you and your process, ensuring your needs and preferences are prioritized.

Post-Birth Assistance:

Support in securing accommodations for delivery, completing passport documents, and referrals for parentage documents as needed.

International Client Support:

Guidance on entering Canada, including navigating current policies and procedures.

Gestures of Appreciation:

Assistance in sending gifts or flowers to your surrogate, at your discretion.

Our goal is to make your surrogacy journey as smooth and fulfilling as possible, providing unwavering support from your first inquiry to welcoming your child.
