We here at #CSOFamily want you to know us.. just as you would your own family!

So, We are bringing to you: Meet the Team Tuesday!

It only seems fitting that I go first so with that:

HI! I’m Robyn! I am currently the Managing Partner here at Canadian Surrogacy Options and Little Miracles – together known as our CSOFamily!

I have been in the Surrogacy World since I was 4… Yup, a whopping 33 years! To say Surrogacy, Egg Donation and Alternative Family building is in my blood would be an understatement. It is my entire life! I live it and breath it daily – helping couples navigate this journey; helping women feel empowered by what their body can do and give is the very center of my core being.

For those that maybe don’t know, my mom Joanne was the very first surrogate in Canada – which led to many funny and likely awkward conversations in school! Alas, those are stories for another day 😉

For me, growing up, Surrogacy has always been ‘normal’. There was never a stigma to it – it just ‘was’. Into my teenage and early adult years, I actually took a different career path than my Mom. It wasn’t until my husband and I had a health scare, that led to a fertility scare and surgery in my early 20’s that really brought me face-to-face with the reality of what my Mom actually did in her daily life… We had a doctor say to us (when I was 22 years old…) “If you were planning on having kids, I’d urge you to start trying now”.

We welcomed our first born, a son, about 18 months later… We were grateful, we were thrilled – we knew we had to try to help others as much as we could!

Through her professional network of amazing contacts, I became an Egg Donor..! With my husband by my side, my mom helping with every cycle and my dad to help give injections, it was just so natural to go through and help these other couples!

3 of these cycles were anonymous and I can only hope that they are living their best life!

I knew I didn’t want to go back to my career life after seeing what we had and what we gone through.. One night, after many talks, My Mom had the bestest idea for us to open Little Miracles. With her guiding hand, experience and gentle nudge of advocacy, Little Miracles was born! Knowing we could make an actual difference in Canada for couples and women wanting to become Egg Donors, we became the first Donor Agency in 2007. As she paved the way for all for Surrogacy in Canada, together we blazed through to support everyone and anyone with Both Surrogacy and Egg Donation. An unstoppable team!

I could write forever about our history, our working lives and our relationship but we may need way more space than what we have here!

In 2013, we learned horrible news .. My Mom was diagnosed with a lung disease. The only cure? A lung transplant. We rallied, we remained positive, and we got to work on a temporary transition to allow her to focus on her health and eventual recovery. She was patient with me, she took her time with planting seeds into my mind to ensure my mind would work as hers did.

Her health took a turn downwards fairly quickly in 2016. She had more bad days than good days and was listed for a transplant. We received this amazing gift in December 2016.

Long story short: The next 3 years were a blur… More on that full story to come!

Sadly, we lost our Matriach December 20, 2019. My mom passed away peacefully with us, her family, holding her hand and by her side. That day, I not only lost my ‘business’ partner, but I lost my best friend.

Our motto has always been Onwards and Upwards. I know she wants us to continue to do great things, to help as many as we possibly can and to continue to Support one another.

Over the last 4 years, I’ve had such a fire burning inside me – to do more, to do better and to help. This fire has only intensified in the last 3 months, knowing she is still guiding me and leading me forward.

Maybe this was a bit long for a “Meet the Team Tuesday”.. But at CSOFamily, I want you to feel as though you are a part of my family. And, to me, the only way for that to start is if you know the whole me too.

As for knowing more about me personally, I live in Cambridge, Ontario – with my husband of almost 15 years and our 2 beautiful children (Cameron who is 14 now and Erica who is 10!). We also have our FurPup Trace; 2 Ninja Cats Molly and Mason; 2 Fishies and our wee baby bird Apollo!

We like to travel when we can and when we can’t, we head to PortaBackyarda – our own private oasis in our backyard! We are a pretty low-key family that just enjoys the simple life of ‘home’ and togetherness!
