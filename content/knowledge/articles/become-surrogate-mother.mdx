If you’re reading this post, you are probably considering becoming a surrogate mother, and that’s so wonderful. But you likely have many questions as many women do when they start thinking about

how to become a surrogate mother

We wanted to remind you of a few things to think about as you make your decision to become a surrogate. We hope you find this helpful as you start your surrogacy journey, and if there is anything you would like to add about your own decision of becoming a surrogate, please leave us a comment!

First things first – always discuss your decision with your

family members and loved ones

. They are the ones who will support you through your journey.

Think about all the wonderful things associated with being a surrogate, and how you are helping another family in need. Our last

blog post

outlined some of these.

If you love being pregnant, then surrogacy is right for you! This one of the biggest factors of becoming a surrogate.

You really must love being pregnant

. Most surrogates are women who are finished growing their families but love being pregnant. It’s an amazing thing to do for couple who want a child more than anything, but just cannot grow a family on their own.

Weigh out the options involved. Did you know there are different

types of surrogacy

? It’s important to do as much research as possible on surrogacy so you are prepared when you meet with your agency. There are some things you will want to consider, such as who you are comfortable being a surrogate for, and which

type of surrogacy

you feel comfortable with as well.

Consult with a

surrogacy agency

like

Canadian Surrogacy Options

. We will meet with you to discuss everything about surrogacy and present you with thoughts about surrogacy you may not have thought about during your own decision process. We even offer counselling and support groups to ensure that your surrogacy journey is comfortable and you feel supported.

We know that becoming a surrogate is a big decision, even when the idea of it feels natural. But as a

surrogacy agency in Canada

, Canadian Surrogacy Options is committed to the success of your journey and your well-being. We will support you all the way! If you have any questions regarding Canadian surrogacy, please contact us!
