The decision to become a surrogate is a big one. It’s a life altering decision that involves the support of many people in your life – but there are so many wonderful positive things associated with it! If you have been thinking about becoming a surrogate, you have probably read a lot of information, but perhaps you haven’t thought about the heart-warming emotional aspects of your decision.

Here are 5 reasons why you should become a surrogate:

You are giving the gift of life to a family

Giving the

gift of life

is an amazing experience. There are many couples who cannot have a child together for several reasons. Being a surrogate is giving these couples something they want very badly but cannot do alone, and you are contributing to something so important and special to another family.

You are helping families in different dynamics who cannot produce

Same sex couples who want to have a family often turn to surrogacy so

they can have a child of their own

. Your commitment to surrogacy for these couples is allowing them to experience the gift of life when they otherwise would not have been able to.

It’s a special way to give back

Not everyone can donate money, but they can volunteer their time. Being a surrogate is a very special way for you, or you and your family to

give back to another family

. There are many families who rely on the process of surrogacy to help them complete their family. Without your commitment, their family would be incomplete.

It is an emotionally satisfying experience

The emotional satisfaction you will get from providing a couple with a way to complete their family is incomparable to anything else. You are providing them with a solution to something that is wanted very much. Throughout the process you will experience excitement, love, joy and many other positive feelings with the couple you are being a surrogate for.

It’s an amazing emotional journey that will be unforgettable

It is a rewarding experience you can share with another family

There are many different ways you can give back in life, but nothing is more rewarding than being able to provide the gift of life. You have the opportunity to see how important this experience is for a couple, and

how much happiness a child that you helped provide them with brings to their lives.

We understand that becoming a surrogate is a major decision that will impact your life in several ways. But as you process your decision, remember all the amazing emotional aspects tied to the process and experience of being a surrogate. CSO is available to guide you through each step, and we are available to answer questions as you make your decision.
