## Here's how it actually works.

When a surrogate is ready to match, we don't start with feelings. We start with foundation. The big things that have to be in place before anything else matters. Values. Family structure. Medical expectations. Lifestyle. Beliefs about pregnancy and parenting. Dealbreakers on both sides. We go through all of it carefully, methodically, because we've been doing this long enough to know that a beautiful connection built on a shaky foundation will crack under the pressure of a journey this long and this intimate.

Once we know the foundation is solid, we step back.

We give the surrogate the profiles. And we let her read.

And here is what happens every single time, without fail. She's reading. Scanning. Nodding. And then she stops.

Not because of the big stuff. Because of something tiny. Something so specific it almost feels silly to say out loud.

You both love the same movie nobody else has ever heard of. Her idea of a perfect Saturday is exactly yours. You named your childhood dog the same name she named hers.

Something clicks. And she picks up the phone and calls us and says the same thing every single time:

*"Those are my people."*

Every time. Magic. Without fail.
